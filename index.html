WHAT ARE COOL MonkeS?

Cool Monkes is an interactive PvE collection of 10,000 randomly generated NFTs on the Ethereum Blockchain, assembled from over 250 unique hand-drawn traits. All Monkes have a variety of outfits, faces, hats, and colors. Cool Monkes gives holders the power to breed and evolve Baby Cool Monkes through an interactive and fun NFT Game.

How Cool is my monke?

All Monkes are hand drawn with love and care, and all are cool, but some are cooler than others….and some are just bananas. Each Monke can have up to 4 traits, from 8 different rarities. Common items like a tank top or sunglasses are less rare, while a UFO or dinosaur outfit is rarer. The rarity of a Monke determines how many $CMB it can earn through staking.
